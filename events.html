Project Streedhan in Collaboration with Girlup India


GirlUp Ludhiana collaborated with Girlup India & Streedhan India to work on 'Project Period' - a month-long campaign that pushed for sustainable menstrual health practices, raised funds for the purpose and also advocated for including that in the national guidelines. Girlup Ludhiana was provided this opportunity by Girlup India to become a part of this project and for its active advocacy on menstruation related awareness. A lot of work was done through our social media handles to spread awareness during this project.

Women Of Today- (Orange The World)


The 16 days between 25 November, the International Day for the Elimination of Violence against Women, and 10 December, Human Rights Day, are known as the 16 Days of Activism against Gender-Based Violence Campaign , a time to galvanize action to end violence against women and girls around the world.
This year's theme for the same was " Orange the world " as the colour "orange" symbolizes a brighter future and solidarity in eliminating all forms of violence .
Girl Up Ludhiana , in unanimity with the campaign, made its logo 'Orange' to mark an end to Gender - based violence , deeply rooted in our society and created cognizance for the same through its posts and hosted a Global Panel Discussion: Women Of Today . It demonstrated the causes and issues of violence against women persisting all over the world and suggested means to end it .
